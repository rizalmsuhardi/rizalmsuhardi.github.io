Profile

About

I am a Research Assistant in the Biology Department at National Sun Yat-sen University (NSYSU), Kaohsiung, Taiwan. My research lives at the meeting point of plant genetics, molecular evolution, conservation genetics, and biology education.

I aspire to grow into an excellent researcher, and I try to bring a hardworking, eager-to-learn spirit to every project — from the careful reading of DNA to the broader question of how science education can serve people and protect nature.
