Profile

About

Rizal M. Suhardi is a Research Assistant in the Biological Sciences Department at National Sun Yat-sen University (NSYSU), Kaohsiung, Taiwan. His research focuses on plant genetics, molecular evolution, conservation genetics, and biology education — with particular expertise in phylogenetics, DNA barcoding, and population genetics of plant and marine species.

Committed to rigorous scientific inquiry, he seeks to bridge computational and experimental approaches in understanding biodiversity, while contributing to conservation efforts and the advancement of biology education.
